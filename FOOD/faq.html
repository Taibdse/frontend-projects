General Questions

All you need to know about VeganFood Restaurant and our services.

  • Do you take reservations?+
    • Though most vegan diners and restaurants don’t take reservations, we do. We take limited number of reservations for groups of 6 - 10 people for dinner and weekday breakfast/lunch. Please note: out of respect for all guests wishing to enjoy an experience with us, we don't seat incomplete parties. Reservations are held for 15 minutes beyond the reserved time, then released to other guests.
  • Do you offer organic products?+
    • All our dairy and bakery products are organic. If the quality, availability and price are right, we do. But top quality is absolutely essential. And wherever possible, we use local or regional produce.
  • How often do you change the menu?+
    • We change our dishes every day. In our menu you'll find over 40 delicious salads, hot dishes, snacks and desserts - as well as baked goods, sandwiches and soups from the counter.
  • Where can I get your recipes?+
    • We're very proud of our cookbook "VeganFood Recipes" which you can get online and in the restaurant. We also share new recipes with you via our blog and in our newsletter.
